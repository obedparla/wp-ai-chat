# Plugin Name — Decision & Research Log

Naming the paid WooCommerce AI chatbot plugin (floating chat widget: answers shopper
questions, searches products, recommends, manages cart; streams AI responses).

---

## ✅ DECISION: **WpStoreChat**

- **Consumer-facing brand:** **StoreChat** (drop/soften "Wp" in the visible brand & logo; keep `wp`/`wpstorechat` only in the plugin slug if useful).
- **Tagline carries the keywords:** *"StoreChat — AI shopping assistant & chatbot for WooCommerce."* (AI + chatbot + WooCommerce live in the tagline/landing copy, NOT the brand.)
- **Why it won:** decisive winner of the buyer "wallet" user-test — clarity lowers perceived purchase risk, which is what converts. See results below.

### Open follow-ups before committing
1. **Trademark check** (USPTO/EUIPO, Nice class 9 / 42) on "StoreChat" / "WpStoreChat".
2. **Mitigate the "WP Store Locator" collision** — that incumbent (50k+ installs) owns "WP Store…" searches; this is the main reason to soften "Wp" in the visible brand.
3. **Grab domains** (all currently free): `wpstorechat.com` (unregistered), `wpstorechat.ai`, `storechat.ai`. Note `storechat.com` is taken/parked.
4. Accept it's a low-ownability/generic name → differentiate with a distinct wordmark + tagline; don't rely on the name for organic discovery.

---

## The user-test that decided it (13 agents)

**Buyer panel:** 10 distinct WooCommerce-owner personas, each voting 10× across wallet/purchase
criteria (trust-with-card, worth-a-premium, longevity, buy-now, etc. — not aesthetics), name
order rotated to kill bias, no tools. **100 ballots.**

| Name | Wallet votes | "Would buy" (of 10) |
|---|---|---|
| **WpStoreChat** | **66** | **8** |
| WpAskStore | 19 | 2 |
| WpStoreScout | 15 | **0** |

StoreChat won 8 of 10 criteria, incl. the decisive ones: Clarity 8–2–0 · Longevity 9–1–0 ·
Legit-not-spammy 9–1–0 · Word-of-mouth 9–1–0 · Buy-now 8–2–0. Repeated buyer reason:
*"I instantly know what I'm paying for."*

**Scout paradox:** swept "worth a premium" **10–0–0** but got **0 actual purchases** — sounds
premium, but nobody could tell what it does, so it never converted. Retired.

**Analyst panel (3 web researchers) ranked the OPPOSITE** (top picks: AskStore ×2, Scout ×1,
StoreChat ×0): StoreChat is the least *ownable/discoverable* (generic, collides with WP Store
Locator + the "…Chat" category, hard to trademark, "chat" seen as fading). **This is the core
trade:** the clarity that wins the sale (StoreChat) is the same genericness that hurts
ownership/SEO. Resolved in StoreChat's favor because it's **paid & sold off-site** — we drive
our own traffic, so point-of-sale conversion matters more than organic name discovery.

---

## Key constraints & learnings (the rules we settled)

1. **🚫 No "Woo".** Automattic trademark — can't register/defend, ad/processor friction, UDRP risk. All `Woo*` names killed.
2. **"WP" is legally fine** (Foundation policy frees "WP"), BUT: wp.org flags/pends plugins whose *name starts with* "WP" → **moot here** since we're paid-only / not on the directory. Still, soften "Wp" in the visible brand for distinctiveness.
3. **Paid-only, no wp.org directory.** Discovery = Google + AI answer engines + listicles + word-of-mouth, not directory keyword search. Rewards distinctive/ownable names — but we're overriding that with the wallet-conversion signal.
4. **"AI" goes in the tagline, not the name.** Dates fast, dilutes, hard to trademark (ChatGPT itself refused as descriptive), "AI-washing" trust tax. Competitors (WoowBot, Tidio, AeroChat, StoreAgent) all keep AI in the descriptor.
5. **Root word: Store > Shop > Cart.** "Shop" collides with Shopify's "Shop"/shop.app brand; "Cart" under-reaches (reads as checkout/abandonment); "Store" = US buyer-intent + premium + collision-free.
6. **Function word: Chat/Bot beat "AI" for clarity; Chat beats Bot** — "Bot" is collision-poisoned (Google StoreBot crawler, eBay/Shopify ShopBot); "Chat" is clean and is the word owners search.
7. **Clarity wins wallets** (the user-test's central finding); distinctiveness wins ownership. For a paid, traffic-driven funnel we chose clarity.

## ⚠️ Competitor / collision watch (live products found while vetting)

Direct or adjacent competitors + namesakes to be aware of (and for positioning):

- **StoreAgent** (`storeagent.ai`, wp.org) — closest direct competitor ("WooCommerce AI Chatbot & Content Tools").
- **WoowBot / WPBot** (QuantumCloud) — most-installed WooCommerce/WP chatbots.
- **StoreMind**, **CartChat** (`cartchat.ai` — this exact product for Shopify), **HeyCart** (`heycart.ai`).
- **BuyScout** — "AI Shopping Assistant" on G2/Product Hunt; shadows all *Scout names.
- **Selli "AI Shop Assistant"**, **Shopify "Shop" / shop.app/ai** — why "Shop" is risky.
- **WP Store Locator** (50k+ installs) — owns "WP Store…" searches; the StoreChat prefix collision.
- **AskMyStore / AskShop.ai / AskTimmy** — the crowded "Ask+store/shop" lane (AskStore risk).
- **Store Scout** (Monash nutrition app) — namesake collision for the bare "Store Scout".

## Rejected (don't re-propose)

- **All `Woo*`** (trademark): WooGenie, WooWhiz, WooScout, WooBot, WooChat, AskWoo, WooMate, etc.
- **Generic keyword strings** (unbrandable/untrademarkable): "WooCommerce AI Chatbot", WooAIChat, WooSalesBot, WooSalesAI ("WooSales AI Chat Assistant" exists), WpStoreChatbot-as-only-option.
- **Taken WP-prefixed:** WPBot, WPGenie, WPClerk, WPButler, WPSage, WPSidekick, WPWizard, WPWhiz, WPPilot, WPConcierge, WPShopper.
- **Taken roots:** Clerkly, Shelfie, Tendly, Storely, Sello, Vendly, StoreTender, ShopHand, ShopBot (eBay/Shopify/CNC), StoreBot (Google StoreBot crawler), ShopChat (Shopify "Shop Chat"), CartScout/CartGenie/CartKeeper/CartAlly, StoreScout (Monash), CartChat (Shopify).
- **User-disliked:** anything with **Whiz**; "Scout"/"Assistant" alone (opaque — don't say what it does, lost the wallet test).

- **R-2026-06-18 — 3 new names challenged the decision, all lost.** 10-buyer wallet panel (rotated order) on WpStoreChat / WpAskStore / WpSalesAgent / WpStoreAssistant / WpAISalesAssistant.

## Round (2026-06-18): WpSalesAgent / WpStoreAssistant / WpAISalesAssistant tested — none beat WpStoreChat

| Name | Would-buy (/10) | #1 picks | Avg |
|---|---|---|---|
| **WpStoreChat** | **8** | **5** | **7.2** |
| WpStoreAssistant (new) | 5 | 2 | 6.7 |
| WpAskStore | 1 | 0 | 6.3 |
| WpSalesAgent (new) | 3 | 1 | 5.6 |
| WpAISalesAssistant (new) | 2 | 2 | 5.4 |

- **WpStoreChat reproduced its 8/10 would-buy** — only name nobody called spammy. Decision holds.
- Panel split: clarity/trust buyers (6/10) → StoreChat; conversion-chasers (3/10) → Sales/Agent names. The Sales/AI names that win the chasers (8–9) actively repel the other 7 ("pushy car-dealer," "word salad," "reeks of hype"). High ceiling, low floor.
- **WpStoreAssistant = strongest newcomer**, displaced WpAskStore as the #2 (won premium + apparel buyers). Possible new fallback in place of WpAskStore — but see collision below.
- **WpAskStore collapsed** under this field (1/10, "sounds like a search box").

**Competitor/branding kills (independent of the vote):**
- **WpAISalesAssistant** ❌ — verbatim the tagline of live rivals **MaxSellora** & **ConvertyBot** ("AI Sales Assistant … for WooCommerce"); breaks no-AI-in-brand rule; 4 words, untrademarkable.
- **WpSalesAgent** ❌ — near-exact collision with live **"Ai Sales Agent (ASA)"** WP.org plugin + **StoreAgent**; "agent" is the saturated 2026 buzzword, will date like "AI".
- **WpStoreAssistant** ⚠️ — collides with WooCommerce's own **"AI Shopping Assistant"** marketplace product; "Assistant" generic/unbrandable (every rival uses it as descriptor). Clear but low-ownability.

**Verdict: keep WpStoreChat.** If a documented #2 is wanted, WpStoreAssistant > WpAskStore (vet the WooCommerce "AI Shopping Assistant" collision first).
